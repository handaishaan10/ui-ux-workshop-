About the Workshop

Unlock the secrets of exceptional user interface and user experience design. This intensive 4-day workshop is crafted for aspiring designers, developers, and anyone eager to build intuitive and delightful digital products. From foundational principles to advanced techniques and a hands-on project, you'll gain the skills and confidence to create impactful designs.

  • Navigation: Principles of intuitive user flows
  • Basic Figma Tools: Your design playground
  • Layout: Structuring your designs effectively
  • Doubt Session

Day 2: Visual Elements & Theory

  • Vectors and Raster: Understanding image types
  • Grouping, Hierarchy: Organizing information visually
  • Color Theory: Psychology and application
  • Typography: Making text readable and beautiful
  • Doubt Session

Day 3: Advanced Techniques & Project Kickoff

  • Important Plugins: Boosting your workflow
  • Gradients, Effects: Adding depth and appeal
  • Home Project: Start building your masterpiece
  • Doubt Session

Day 4: Project Showcase & Beyond

  • Project Review: Constructive feedback
  • Fixing Mistakes: Polishing your work
  • Surprise Project: A new challenge awaits!
  • Farewell & Networking
